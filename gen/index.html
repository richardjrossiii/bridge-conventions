General notes on 1M:

Opener's hand is limited, so our raise structure is a bit different from normal 2/1 systems.

Important note: Reverses are alertable, and non-forcing, showing only extreme shape. Care should be taken when reversing if responder has a potentially weak hand (especially after forcing 1NT).

In competition (except after double), 3M is strictly weak, and we re-purpose the bid immediately before as a mixed raise.

In addition, new suits at the two level are negative.
